About Us

A trusted merchant house for the yarn trade

Evitaas Merchants is the link between the mills that spin yarn and the manufacturers who turn it into fabric. We work across the textile value chain — cotton, synthetics, yarn, gray fabric — as trader, broker, and commission agent, sourcing and supplying on behalf of both buyers and sellers, across India.
