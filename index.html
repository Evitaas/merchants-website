About Us

A trusted merchant house for the yarn trade

Evitaas Merchants operates as a trader, broker, and commission agent across the textile value chain — from raw cotton and synthetic fibers through to yarn, gray fabric, and finished goods. Based in Ahmedabad, we work in India and internationally, sourcing, supplying, and dealing in textile materials on behalf of both buyers and sellers.

Our core business is yarn: connecting the textile mills that spin yarn from raw cotton with the fabric manufacturers who turn that yarn into gray fabric. Every trade we facilitate draws on relationships built and tested over years in the market.
